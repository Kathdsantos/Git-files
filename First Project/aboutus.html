Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng San Pablo is a local university located in the City of Seven Lakes, San Pablo City in the province of Laguna. PSLP has been assisting students in getting started on the path of their dreams since 1997.

The name “Green Mynas” refers to PLSPians who are willing to share their compassion with others, as well as their intelligence and strength in the face of adversity.

Learners have maintained their trust in the institution’s ability to help them grow intellectually, socially, and spiritually, as well as contribute significantly to the future of their community and country.
